About us

One Accord is a prominent company that offers all-around solutions for various Mechatronics and Automation requirements all over India. We use techniques of Design to Manufacture, Reverse Engineering, and Rapid prototyping to provide services such as converting Ideas to Products, Fabrication, Designing, IoT solutions, and many more. We develop innovative, economic, and sustainable solutions in the field of automation, motion control, and robotics and stimulate effective resolutions for mechanical, electronic, electrical, and control engineering, software development, and system integration.
